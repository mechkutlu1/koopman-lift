Koopman Lift
Nonlinear → linear observables

A nonlinear flow becomes an exactly linear system in the right coordinates. Those coordinates are the Koopman eigenfunctions: nonlinear functions of state that nonetheless evolve as pure exponentials. Here the observables x₁, x₂, x₁² span an invariant subspace, so the lift is exact — no approximation, which is what makes the linear model trustworthy for control.
